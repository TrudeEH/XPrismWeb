Our philosophy

We believe that software should focus on simplicity, clarity, and frugality. Our philosophy is to keep things minimal and usable, which we believe should be the mainstream approach in the IT industry.
We aim to prove this with our projects, which are geared towards advanced and experienced computer users, who are often ignored by proprietary software and mainstream open-source projects.
Our goal is to design simple and elegant software that is reliable and maintainable, by striving for minimalism and clarity in our development process.
